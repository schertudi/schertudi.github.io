A SLICK ESCAPE

Developed in 48 hours with one other person, this was our entry for the 2023 Global Game Jam. The theme was "roots", with our interpretation of this being a creature that branches out as it eats food. This turned into a stealth game where you, a science experiment gone wrong, must grow large enough to destroy and escape the lab without anyone noticing.

You can download this game at https://globalgamejam.org/2023/games/slick-escape-0.

My work

  • Branches following mouse
  • Ability to destroy objects in the room
  • Enemy behaviour - entering room at set times and zapping the slime if beyond the caution tape.
